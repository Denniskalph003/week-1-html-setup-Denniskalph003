Ugali

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Chicken

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Sukuma Wiki

  • 1 bunch sukuma wiki (kale), chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Instructions

Ugali

  1. Boil the water in a large pot.
  2. Slowly add maize flour to the boiling water, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Reduce heat to low and continue to stir until the mixture thickens and pulls away from the sides of the pot.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat for 10 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat and let it sit for a few minutes before serving.

Chicken

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Add the tomatoes, salt, pepper, and curry powder. Stir well and cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  5. Cover and simmer on low heat until the chicken is fully cooked.

Sukuma Wiki

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add the chopped sukuma wiki and salt. Stir well and cook until the greens are wilted and tender.

Enjoy your meal!
